Raku Raku Kinen Appli Wii: Kinenka no Isha ga Oshieru Nanoka de Yamaru Hōhō

Raku Raku Kinen Appli Wii: Kinenka no Isha ga Oshieru Nanoka de Yamaru Hōhō (roughly translated as Easy Smoking Cessation App Wii: How to Quit Smoking in 7 Days, Taught by a Smoking Cessation Specialist) is an application first released for the WiiWare service on July 14, 2009. It was developed by SilverStar and is a title designed to help users quit smoking.

Mii usage

Mii characters are used as 3D icons to represent the user. They do not show expressions and will appear in the top left corner of the screen during the game's main menu and lessons.

Circus

Circus is a party video game first released for the Wii only in Europe on April 30, 2010. It is a collection of motion controlled minigames themed around a circus.

Mii usage

Circus uses Mii characters as 2D profile icons for each registered player.

Oddities

  • Mii characters will be rendered with a sad expression until they are picked, a neutral expression when the cursor hovers over them, and a happy expression if selected.

Billy's Boot Camp: Wii de Enjoy Diet

Billy's Boot Camp: Wii de Enjoy Diet is a rhythm fitness video game first released for the Wii only in Japan on April 21, 2011. The title was developed by Rocket and is a spinoff of their Shape Boxing series. The game stars fitness guru Billy Blanks and is themed around his Billy's Bootcamp Tae-Bo exercise program.

Mii usage

Mii characters appear as background characters in Billy's Boot Camp: Wii de Enjoy Diet and can be seen following Billy Blanks' exercises in the background of gameplay along with the game's original characters. The player must complete a set number of exercise sessions to have Mii characters present.

AR Games

AR Games is a collection of seven minigames that utilize augmented reality technology. It is pre-installed on every Nintendo 3DS system and uses six cards (called AR Cards) that come included with the system.

Mii usage

Mii characters are used in the Mii Pics mode where they can be freely moved around the AR Card's play space. Players can set their Miis' poses, expressions, and size and take pictures that are saved to the SD card / Nintendo 3DS Camera app. Up to twenty Miis can be used.

The mode is unlocked by completing the first section of the title's archery minigame.

Dance Dance Revolution: Disney Grooves

Dance Dance Revolution: Disney Grooves is a rhythm video game released only in North America for the Wii on April 2nd, 2009. It was developed by Barnhouse Effect, Disney Interactive Studios, and Konami. It is the second game in the Dance Dance Revolution series with Mii support and the first Disney related game to use them.

Mii usage

A Mii character can be unlocked as a selectable dancer and can be customized with outfits and costumes based on Disney or Dance Dance Revolution characters. They are unlocked by completing by a request in the Fantasy Fun Time venue within Groove Mode; where the player is challenged by a Guest Mii (Guest E) in a dance-off. Miis can also be used in the My Studio mode which allows players to take pictures of their Mii interacting with different Disney characters.

Oddities

  • Guest E, the Mii who challenges you in a dance-off, will be seen wearing a baby costume rather than the game's default outfit for Miis.

Trivia

Kumamon Bomber Puzzle de Kumamon Taisou

Kumamon Bomber Puzzle de Kumamon Taisou is a tile-matching puzzle video game released only in Japan for the Nintendo 3DS on March 13, 2014. The title was developed by Rocket and features the Japanese mascot Kumamon traveling around Japan to make people happy.

Mii usage

Mii characters are supporting characters in this title. The player's personal Mii is seen on the title screen and throughout gameplay dancing and traveling with Kumamon. A successful combo or special move performed will cause Miis stored on the system to appear in the background and dance along. The color of Miis' shirts will also differ based on their favorite color.

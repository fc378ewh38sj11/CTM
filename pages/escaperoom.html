Real Dasshutsu Game x Nintendo 3DS: Chou Hakai Keikaku Kara no Dasshutsu

Real Dasshutsu Game x Nintendo 3DS: Chou Hakai Keikaku Kara no Dasshutsu (translated as Real Escape Game x Nintendo 3DS: Escape from the Plan of Mega Destruction) is a downloadable Nintendo 3DS title developed by indieszero and Nintendo in collaboration with SCRAP; a Japanese escape room game chain known for their Real Escape Room games. It was released on July 31, 2015 only in Japan.

The game is essentially an escape room that takes place inside the user's Nintendo 3DS system. Players must track down a bomb inside their system and stop it in time by solving puzzles within a darker replica of the Home Menu and its built-in applications.

Mii usage

Mii characters appear in certain segments of the game. Players are required to go through the game's replica Friend List app where unique Miis with the same favorite color must be tapped on in order to progress. The player must also visit Miiverse to grab a tile where the five loading screen Miis are present. Since the game supports online multiplayer, Miis are also used as icons to identify fellow players during gameplay.

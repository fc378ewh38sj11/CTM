007: Quantum of Solace

007: Quantum Of Solace is a shooter video game first released for the Wii on October 31, 2008 based on the James Bond movie franchise; specifically the films Casino Royale (2006) and Quantum of Solace (2008).

Mii usage

007: Quantum Of Solace uses Mii characters as 2D profile icons for each save file. Players also have the option to select this icon to use in the game's online multiplayer mode.

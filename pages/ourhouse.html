Our House: Party!

Our House: Party! is a party video game released only in North America for the Wii on September 2, 2009. It was developed by Budcat Creations and is a successor to Our House for the Nintendo DS.

The title is notable for its heavy product placement of the American retail chain The Home Depot; with certain physical copies including a gift card to the chain.

Mii usage

Mii characters are seen as background characters during certain minigames and the game's free roam mode. In the suburban neighborhood the game takes place in, Miis can be seen washing a car, delivering the mail, and mimicking a mime. They are also seen in each playthrough's introduction minigame where one Mii will be seen shopping at The Home Depot.

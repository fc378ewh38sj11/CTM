MiiEdit (Nintendo Switch)

The Nintendo Switch's Mii creation system (internally known as MiiEdit) is the fourth major piece of Mii creation software releasing with the Nintendo Switch on March 3, 2017.


General information / changes from past editors

  • First Mii editor to be stored within system settings rather than a dedicated app.

  • First Mii editor with no native background music. Since the editor is now an applet, compatible software will have its background music play while a user creates a Mii.

  • 92 colors for hair, eyes, facial hair, glasses, and lips have been added.

  • 9 new glasses have been added.

  • Copying, favorite, creator's name, and birthday options have been removed; bringing down Mii data files by 8 bytes.

  • Mii limit has dropped to 100.

  • Miis now appear on the right of the screen during creation.

  • Choose a Look-Alike returns from the Wii. Note this could be accessed on the Wii U through its first-time setup, but does not appear again in its Mii Maker application.

  • Amiibo data can be scanned to copy the Mii stored on it.

  • Create from Photo option has been removed due to the Nintendo Switch lacking a camera.

  • Users can no longer generate and scan QR codes or renders of Miis.
